Frame Work

 We help you determine your organization’s strategic scheme designed to help administer and coordinate business functions regarding the human capital.Our services include :
  • Creating departments or divisions
  • Formulating policies & procedures
  • Designing internal process
  • Setting-up hierarchy, authority & responsibility
  • Fixing key result areas (kra’s)
  • Creating job descriptions
  • Developing job grades & pay scales
  • Preparing plan, budget & reports

Recruitment

  • Setting goals & key performance indicators (kpi’s)
  • Conducting performance assessments
  • Facilitating performance improvement program (pip)
  • Managing career advancements
  • Designing and administering performance rewards
  • Managing succession
