Frame Work

Devising and managing the structure which includes –> creating departments or divisions -> formulating policies & procedures -> designing internal process -> setting-up hierarchy, authority & responsibility -> fixing key result areas (kra’s) -> creating job descriptions -> developing job grades & pay scales -> preparing plan, budget & reports.

Recruitment

Managing the recruitment activities which includes -> candidate sourcing -> tracking of applicants -> conducting preliminary screenings & selection tests -> coordinating interviews -> conducting background verifications -> coordinating medical tests -> providing pre-employment counselling -> handling employment offer & acceptance.

Administration

Managing administrative activities which includes-> maintaining employee database-> facilitating on-boarding process-> handling payroll process-> filing statutory returns-> managing pay revisions & increments-> administering employee benefits-> provisioning welfare measures-> processing requests & queries-> facilitating engagement activities-> renewing licences & registrations-> maintaining statutory records-> liaisoning statutory & legal matters-> managing compliance-> handling off-boarding process (exits & settlements)

Performance

Managing and cultivating a high performance culture through various activities which includes -> setting goals & key performance indicators (kpi’s) -> conducting performance assessments -> facilitating performance improvement program (pip) -> managing career advancements -> Designing and administering performance rewards ->.

Training

Managing the learning process for skill improvement and growth which includes -> handling on-boarding trainings -> conducting capability assessments -> facilitating skill & compliance training -> measuring effectiveness through training evaluations..
